# Privacy Policy

**LovableBuilder Claude Plugin**
**Last updated: March 2026**

---

## Overview

LovableBuilder is a Claude Code plugin built and maintained by Agent Builder Academy (jonathan@agentbuilder.academy). This privacy policy explains what data the plugin does and does not collect when you use it.

---

## What This Plugin Does Not Collect

LovableBuilder does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. Specifically:

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
- We do not track your usage, sessions, or activity
- We do not send any data to external servers or third-party services
- We do not store any conversations, prompts, or outputs from your Claude sessions
- We do not use cookies or any form of tracking technology

---

## How the Plugin Works

LovableBuilder operates entirely within your local Claude Code environment. It consists of:

- **Skill files** (SKILL.md) — plain text instruction files read by Claude at runtime
- **Reference files** — markdown documents containing templates and guidance
- **Command files** — markdown files that trigger specific workflows

All processing happens locally through Claude Code and Anthropic's Claude AI. No data leaves your machine via this plugin.

---

## Third-Party Services

When you use LovableBuilder, you interact with:

- **Anthropic / Claude** — your conversations and prompts are processed according to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- **Lovable.dev** — any content you enter into Lovable is subject to [Lovable's Privacy Policy](https://lovable.dev/privacy)

We have no control over and take no responsibility for the data practices of these third-party services.
